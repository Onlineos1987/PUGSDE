Our Mission

Why we do what we do

"Delight people with interactive music experiences"

At Amanotes, we believe that music is for everyone. Our mission is to create innovative music games that allow people to interact with music in ways they never thought possible.

Founded in 2014, we've grown to become the world's leading music game publisher by combining cutting-edge technology with creative gameplay and popular music.

With over 3.5 billion downloads across our portfolio of games, we continue to push the boundaries of what's possible in mobile music gaming.
